Activities

DANCE

Dance, the movement of the body in a rhythmic way, usually to music and within a given space, for the purpose of expressing an idea or emotion, releasing energy, or simply taking delight in the movement itself. Some might enjoy it, some might be indifferents some might love it and some might hate it. (of a person) move in a quick and lively way.

MUSIC

Music is sound that has been organized by using rhythm, melody or harmony.Tunes are made of notes that go up or down or stay on the same pitch. Music often has rhythm. Rhythm is the way the musical sounds and silences are put together in a sequence. It's a language of emotion in that it can represent different feelings and barge into the soul with no limitations.

ART

Art, in its broadest sense, is a form of communication. It means whatever the artist intends it to mean, and this meaning is shaped by the materials, techniques, and forms it makes use of, as well as the ideas and feelings it creates in its viewers.
